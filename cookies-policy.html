WHAT ARE COOKIES?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit a website. They serve various purposes, such as recognizing you as a user, remembering your preferences, and providing a personalized browsing experience. Cookies may be placed by us (first-party cookies) or by third parties on our behalf (third-party cookies).

  6. To comply with legal and regulatory requirements

TYPES OF COOKIES WE USE

Essential Cookies: Essential cookies are required for our Site to operate. They allow you to navigate our Site and use its services and features (e.g., cookies that help you stay logged in). Without essential cookies, our Site will not run smoothly. Our Site (or certain services or features) might not even be available to you simply because of technical limitations.

Preference/Functionality Cookies: Preference cookies allow us to store information about your choices, settings and preferences. They also help us recognize you when you return to our Site, remember your language settings (among others) and customize our Site accordingly. They are not essential to the functioning of our Site.

Analytic/Performance Cookies: Analytics cookies collect or use information about your Site use, which helps us improve our Site. One of the uses of analytics trackers is to show us which pages are most frequently visited, help us record difficulties you have with our Site, track subscription purchases and behaviours leading to subscription purchases, and measure how well ads perform.

Advertising/Targeting Cookies: We work with third-party advertising networks that may use cookies to deliver targeted advertisements to you based on your browsing activities on our Site and other websites. These cookies collect information about your visits to provide relevant ads. You can opt out of targeted advertising by adjusting your cookie preferences.

Third-Party Cookies: Third-party services integrated into our Site, such as social media plugins, may use their own cookies to enhance your interaction with their platforms. We have no control over these cookies and recommend reviewing the respective third-party cookie policies.
